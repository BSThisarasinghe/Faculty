BSc (Applied Sciences) Special in Physical Education

The programmme aims at producing specialists in the field of Physical Education to fulfill the country's requirements in the educational sector. The core subjects such as Physical Education for Schools, Play and Early Childhood Development, Developing skills and Methods for Teaching Physical Education, Administration of Physical Education and Coaching Methods are offered.


BSc (Applied Sciences) Special in Sport Sciences & Management

The programme is designed to fulfill the country's requirement for trained specialists in the Sports Science and Management. The four years of the programme cover general subjects as well as sports subjects. The students will gain theoretical and practical knowledge in the areas of Sports Management and Administration, Sports Psychology, Sports Nutrion, Sports Medicine, Sports Law, Sports Budgeting and Finance, Theories of Training and Coaching Practicing. Practical aspects of all the major forms of sports are also offered throughout the four years. Students can get specialized in a selected sport during the third and fourth years.
