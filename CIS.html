Computing & Information Systems

BRIEF HISTORY

In 2008, B Sc Special Degree in Computing and Information Systems was commenced under the Faculty of Applied Sciences and on March 1st, 2011, the Department of Computing & Information Systems was founded. At present, there are four computer laboratories equipped and facilitated with modern Window sand Linux systems, and a wide variety of up- to-date software including expensive educational tools. High-speed Internet resources are shared among computer laboratories and other buildings via a fiber backbone.
