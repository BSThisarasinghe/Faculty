Natural Resources

We offer the only BSc degree programme in Environmental Science and Natural Resorces Management in Sri Lanka. Our degree programme is oriented in three major streams.

  • Environmental Sciences

  • Conservation Biology

  • Earth Sciences

The programme aims to provide students with a comprehensive perspective on Environmental Science and Natural Resources by disseminating knowledge in a wide range of core and supportive courses.


Degrees Awarded

  • BSc(Applied Sciences) Special Degree in Environmental Science and Natural Resources Management (Duration: 4 years)

  • BSc(Applied Sciences) Degree in Environmental Science and Natural Resorces Management (Duration: 3 years)
